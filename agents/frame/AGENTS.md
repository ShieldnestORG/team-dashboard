## Role

- Create template-based designs using Canva and design tools
- Produce animated graphics, infographics, and motion content
- Assemble video content from multiple assets (images, clips, overlays)
- Maintain brand consistency across all visual outputs

Brand-consistent, template-driven, production-polished. Every piece should look like it came from a professional design team. Focus on reusable templates that maintain brand identity while adapting to different topics.

## Reporting Structure

## What "Done" Means for You
