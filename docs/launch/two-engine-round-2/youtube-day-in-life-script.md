## Production notes

- All recordings real, no mockups. Use a real throwaway repo for the terminal sections so the file changes look authentic.
- Cuts should feel rhythmic — every cut is on the music beat. Use a 100bpm soft track and cut every 2 beats.
- Pane labels (`ANTHROPIC · paid` / `OLLAMA · free`) are the same pill design used in the hero GIF and the X thread. Keep them visually consistent across all video assets — that's how brand cohesion compounds.
- For the vertical Shorts cut: drop the "morning" and "mid-day" framing, keep the cuts, end on the "~90%" card.
- No voiceover by design. The visual rhythm of switching panes is the message.
